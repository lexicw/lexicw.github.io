About Me

Hello, World! My name is Alexandra Wheeler. I have been interested in Web Development since I was a kid, when I taught myself HTML and CSS in my spare time. After doing Order Entry for 6 years, I decided to go back to school full-time for Software Development. I graduated with an Associate of Science in Software Development at Ivy Tech Community College, and then later transferred that degree to Western Governors University, where I got a Bachelor of Science in Software Development.
